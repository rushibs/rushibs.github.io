Who Am I?

I am a graduate student pursuing Master of Science in Mechatronics and Robotics Engineering at NYU Tandon School of Engineering. I have a B.Tech. in Mechanical Engineering from K. J. Somaiya College of Engineering, Mumbai. Currently, I am working as a Graduate Research Assistant at AI4CE (Automation and Intelligence for Civil Engineering) Lab at NYU Tandon School of Engineering under the guidance of Prof. Chen Feng. My current project is based on Collaborative embodied agents benchmark development by developing hardware environment for embodied AI research and implementing state-of-the-art embodied AI algorithms in both simulators and real-world. I love challenges posses a problem-solving mindset by applying innovative ideas. Adding to that, I am a professional National level Badminton player and currently represent NYU at various tournaments.
